About the photographer

Images are life the basic building block for the expression of his creativity for Tobias Herrmann. With young 17 years was born in Berlin began to create short films about life, love and suffering. What began with moving images and most flickered something dark and melancholic about the screen, crystallized to colorful snapshots that are not captured by him with the video, but the SLR. Tobias Herrmann is an observer who tell stories with his pictures and in his portraits would bring new facets to the fore. Him it is always possible to preserve the naturalness of his models, which is not only due to his clean style, but also his penchant for existing daylight.

„It does not work with rigid, predetermined poses. Every model gets to me, so to speak, a special treatment. Until adjusts a familiar atmosphere, must be able to open as a photographer and show empathy. I let my model like free to then naturally capture with the camera.”

As priorities Tobias Herrmann chose the fashion and portrait photography and is predominantly male models and newcomers to the scene.
